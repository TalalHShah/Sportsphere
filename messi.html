Lionel Messi Leads Inter Miami to Victory in MLS Debut

Lionel Messi made an instant impact in his Major League Soccer debut, leading Inter Miami to a thrilling
3-2 victory over LA Galaxy.


Sunday 28 July 2024 11:21, PK

Lionel Messi celebrates after scoring the winning goal for Inter Miami

In a highly anticipated debut, Lionel Messi didn't disappoint as he showcased his talent and leadership on the field, guiding Inter Miami to a hard-fought victory against LA Galaxy.


The match saw Messi scoring a brilliant goal in the 85th minute, securing the win for his team. His presence has already started to make a significant impact, with fans and teammates alike praising his influence both on and off the pitch.

The atmosphere in the stadium was electric, with fans chanting Messi's name and waving banners in his honor. Messi's goal in the 85th minute was a moment of pure brilliance. After receiving a well-timed pass from his teammate, he maneuvered past two defenders with deft footwork and calmly slotted the ball into the bottom corner of the net.
